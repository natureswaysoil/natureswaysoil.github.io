Data Deletion Instructions

At Nature's Way Soil & Vermicompost LLC, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page provides instructions on how to request deletion of your data from our Natures Way Soilzz TikTok application.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your TikTok profile information (username, profile picture, bio)
  • Any content data you've shared with our App
  • Usage data and analytics associated with your account
  • Device information linked to your account
  • Any preferences or settings you've configured
⚠️ Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will need to reconnect your TikTok account if you wish to use the App again in the future.

How to Request Data Deletion

Step 1: Prepare Your Request

Gather the following information:

  • Your TikTok username
  • The email address associated with your TikTok account (if applicable)
  • Any additional details that can help us identify your account
Step 2: Send Deletion Request Email

Send an email to: james@natureswaysoil.com

Use the following email template:

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Natures Way Soilzz App Dear Nature's Way Soil Team, I am requesting the deletion of all my personal data from the Natures Way Soilzz TikTok application. My Details: - TikTok Username: [Your TikTok username] - Email: [Your email address] - App ID: 1888081348440882 I understand that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Thank you, [Your Name]
Step 3: Confirmation

After we receive your request:

  • We will send you a confirmation email within 24-48 hours
  • We may ask for additional verification to ensure the security of your account
  • Once verified, your data deletion will be processed
Step 4: Processing Time

Your data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your verified request. You will receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Alternative Method: In-App Deletion

If available in your version of the App, you can also request data deletion directly:

  1. Open the Natures Way Soilzz App
  2. Go to Settings or Account Settings
  3. Look for "Delete My Data" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be disconnected from the App
  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems
  • Backup copies will be deleted within 90 days
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for legal or analytical purposes
  • You can reconnect your TikTok account at any time to use the App again

Data Retention Exceptions

In certain circumstances, we may need to retain some data:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To resolve disputes or enforce our agreements
  • For fraud prevention and security purposes
  • As required by applicable law

Any retained data will be kept secure and used only for the specified purposes.
